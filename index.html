The process for property registration in Chennai involves the following steps: 1. Verification of property documents and ensuring the title is clear. 2. Preparation of the Sale Deed and getting it stamped by the Sub-Registrar office. 3. Payment of stamp duty and registration fees, which are usually around 7% of the property value for stamp duty and 1% for registration fees. 4. Submitting the Sale Deed and other necessary documents to the Sub-Registrar office for registration. 5. The Sub-Registrar will verify the documents, and upon approval, the property will be registered in the buyer's name.
